A ClearView into study planning

ClearView was founded with one question in mind - how can we make online learning more efficient for students? We found that Learning Management Systems (LMS) have a focus on content hosting, not on the learning experience. This has cost students time and therefore reduced their productivity. It is our mission to solve this pain point for students and also improve student engagement with course content by making study planning more efficient.

We decided that a dashboard was needed to show students a timeline of all their course deliverables and track their progress. All course content relating to release dates and deadlines are uploaded by our learning institute partners, and are presented in a useful timeline with colour indicators to alert students of approaching deadlines. ClearView is also editable by the student to include notes and allow progress updates. This single sign-on, responsive design allows students to check their study progress anytime, anywhere, on any device. Students no longer have to spend unnecessary time searching across multiple pages on their LMS, as the course plan and deliverables are available in ClearView, in one place, from the very first login

Please visit our contact page if you would like to find out more or if you would like an info pack on how to become a partner with ClearView.

'ClearView' was born with one simple goal in mind, Improving the user experience of LMS users worldwide with a simple yet powerful data rich responsive website.
